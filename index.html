Summary of Learning Outcomes

1. Identify and analyse critically security risks, threats and vulnerabilities in information systems, accounting for the current threat landscape.

Evidenced in the initial risk identification report (Unit 6), where I selected and justified ISO/IEC 27005:2018 as the risk assessment methodology for Pampered Pets' current operations, and in the follow-on quantitative report (Unit 11), which enumerated and quantified supply chain and quality risks using a three-point probability model.

2. Gather and synthesise information from multiple sources to aid in the systematic analysis of risks & security issues.

Evidenced by the range of sources used across both reports, including ISO standards, NIST guidance, ENISA's Threat Landscape report, and IBM's Cost of a Data Breach report, all synthesised to support the quantitative risk figures and the disaster recovery strategy.

3. Critically determine appropriate methodologies, tools and techniques to mitigate and/or solve security risks and their business impact.

Evidenced by the selection and justification of Annualised Loss Expectancy and a PERT-based probability model, and by the prioritised, business-benefit-linked recommendations table tying each control to its commercial impact.

4. Articulate the legal, social, ethical, and professional issues faced by information security and risk professionals.

Evidenced by treating GDPR compliance as a mandatory business requirement in the quantitative report, and by the discussion of vendor lock-in trade-offs in the disaster recovery strategy.

Team Meeting Notes

Sopheaktra and I communicated mainly through the module platform and WhatsApp. We agreed a working plan early on (3–4 June): each of us would draft our own section independently, then combine the strongest parts into one report, followed by a joint review before submission. On 5 June, after comparing both of our drafts, I recommended using Sopheaktra's version as the base since it was technically stronger, and suggested three additions: a Business Impact Analysis section, a conclusion paragraph, and an Asset Identification table. Sopheaktra incorporated these on 6 June, and the final report was submitted after I confirmed I was satisfied with the content.
